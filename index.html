Bee Swarm Simulator

Game Description

Bee Swarm Simulator is a ROBLOX game that takes you on an amazing journey. In this ROBLOX game, you hatch bees to collect pollen in order to create honey. Honey is the currency in the game that helps you upgrade your gear to collect more pollen and honey. you can discover new bees, complete quests, fight enemies and explore new areas the more bees you hatch!

Bee Types

Bees are the most important part of the game. (Quite literally says it in the game title.) And there's many types you can hatch! Firstly, there's a big variety of blue bees and red bees and they all serve different purposes. Depending on how many you have at your hive, you might want to prioritize their abilities at different colored fields. If you have many red bees you benefit a lot from red flowers/pollen. Same goes with white bees and blue bees. Each bee always have abilities that they give you depending on their rarity and what field they're in!

Types of Fields

There's a big variety of fields and what type of flowers and pollen you collect in the game. Knowing these fields will help you find the type of field you need for your quests!

When you first start the game, the starter fields are:

Dandelion Field: You can find this right at spawn! This field should have big white dandelions to signify that it has primarily white pollen that you need to collect for quests.

Blue Flower Field: You can find this if you walk a little more around the map (if you haven't accessed other areas depending on the amount of bees you have) Be careful though, there's beetles that attack you so you need strong bees to fight them off.

Clover Field: You can find this field right next to the Blue Flower Field, they are on the side of where the Shop is to buy items.

Mushroom Field: This field is easily found because it has big red spotted mushrooms that you can see easily from spawn, when you spawn in the game, you can walk forward and it'll be right in front of you!

Sunflower Field: This field is easily found right next to the Mushroom Field and the Dandelion Field, you can't neccessarily miss these fields when you first start the game.

Quests and NPC's

There's many quests you can start around the map. When you first start the game you'll come across the first 3 bears (pictures below) until you unlock the new areas. If the bears have an exclamation point in front of them, you know you have a new quest or you completed a quest.

Amulets

Amulets are things you can collect throughout the game and grant several buffs to the player and their bee.They can be gained by completing special challenges or killing high leveled enemies. Ways that you can acquire these amulets is by:

- Fighting King Beetle

- Doing the Ant Farm Challenge

- Fighting the Stump Snail

- Completing the Stick Bug Challenge

- Collecting 100 Moon Charms to convert into a Moon Amulet

Essentially, Amulets in the game are very useful to have, especially for long, tedious quests that ask for you to collect tons of pollen!
